:::note Getting Started Right

- Assess feasibility and resource requirements
- Identify success metrics
- Establish baseline performance
- Plan data requirements

## Building High-Performing Teams

### Key Roles

**Data Scientists** - Model research and experimentation
**ML Engineers** - Production systems and scalability  
**Domain Experts** - Problem understanding and validation  
**Product Managers** - Business alignment and prioritization  
**DevOps/MLOps** - Infrastructure and deployment

:::tip Cross-Functional Collaboration
Align technical solutions with business objectives through regular collaboration between all stakeholders. Set realistic timelines for the experimental nature of AI development.
:::
